Amphi

Scientific Name

Amphitritus Molecula

Discovered by / at

Chinese Marine Biologists in Shanghai, January 13, 2024

Family

Asteriidae

Habitat

The deep sea floor

Appearance

In its infant stages it begins as yellow, pink, and red molecule

Key Characteristics

  • Extremely reactive to environment
  • Scared of sea urchins
  • Curious about humans

What do We Know so Far About Amphi? Simple chemical compound substances evolve Amphis differently. Sugar gives it energy, water makes it grow, and salt make it change colors at random.
Guidelines for Human Interaction with Amphis Amphis have a natural curiosity towards humans and demonstrate no hostility or fear.

  • One mouse press will get Amphi to follow you
  • Pressing the 'S' key will display sugar cubes
  • Pressing 'Q' will display salt grains
  • Pressing 'W' will display water drops
  • If it gets too close to a sea urchin, it will camoflauge itself
